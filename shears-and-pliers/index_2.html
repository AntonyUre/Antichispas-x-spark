Use And Management Of Non Sparking Safety Tools

The non-sparking safety tool is a special safety tool, which has the characteristic of not generating sparks when the tool collides with the workpiece or friction.

What Is the Difference Between a Claw Hammer and a Split Hammer?

The curved claw hammer is a double-headed hammer used to hammer and pull out nails. One end of a curved claw hammer is deeper and the other side is flatter. This is usually used to pull out nails.
